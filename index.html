The Miles Rausch Family 2013

2013 was a maintenance release for us: no new kids, no new jobs, no new dwellings. So what *did* happen this year? Let's take a look at the numbers.

Your subjects:

MILES (man, blue) HOLLI (woman, pink) IAN (man, bright blue) AINSLEY (woman, bright pink)

This was a year for landmark birthdays. Ian and Holli made respectable progress, turning 4 and 27 respectively. Ainsley finally crossed into whole numbers, a feat celebrated with a cathartically destroyed cake. Miles, meanwhile, reluctantly stepped into his third decade, a milestone which was made easier by the fantastic surprise party planned and executed by Holli.
